International IP Tools

International IP Filing Systems

🌐

Madrid System (Trademarks)

File a single international trademark application through WIPO to seek protection in up to 130 member countries. Cost-effective way to build international trademark protection from a single India-based application.

💡

PCT (Patents)

The Patent Cooperation Treaty allows filing of a single international patent application that is effective in 150+ member countries. Provides 30 months from priority date to enter national phases, enabling cost-effective international patent strategy.

🎨

Hague System (Designs)

Register industrial designs in multiple countries through a single international application filed with WIPO. Currently covers 90+ countries and regional design offices, including the EU and USA.
