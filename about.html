About LawNetics

A distinguished Intellectual Property law firm committed to protecting innovation, creativity and business interests across India and globally.

Our Story

Building a Legacy of IP Excellence

LawNetics was established with a singular vision: to make world-class intellectual property legal services accessible to innovators, creators, and businesses across India and beyond. Founded by passionate IP professionals, the firm has grown into a trusted name in the intellectual property landscape.

With offices in Kushinagar and Delhi, LawNetics serves a diverse clientele ranging from startups and entrepreneurs to established corporations and multinational companies. Our attorneys combine deep legal expertise with a thorough understanding of the business context in which IP assets operate.

We are more than just a law firm – we are strategic IP partners who genuinely invest in our clients' success. Whether you are filing your first trademark or managing a complex global patent portfolio, LawNetics brings the same dedication and expertise to every engagement.
